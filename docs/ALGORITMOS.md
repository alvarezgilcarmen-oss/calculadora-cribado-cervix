# Screening Algorithms Documentation

## PDPCCU Screening Algorithm

### Age-Based Flowchart:
- **Ages 21-29**: Pap test every 3 years.
- **Ages 30-65**: 
  - Pap test + HPV test (co-testing) every 5 years, or 
  - Pap test alone every 3 years.
- **Age 65+**: Discontinue screening if history is negative.

### Notes:
- Individuals should consult with their healthcare provider for personalized recommendations.


## AEPCC 2022 Screening Algorithm

### Age-Based Flowchart:
- **Ages 21-29**: Pap test every 3 years.
- **Ages 30-65**: 
  - HPV test every 5 years, or 
  - Pap test + HPV test (co-testing) every 5 years, or 
  - Pap test alone every 3 years.
- **Age 65+**: Discontinue screening if prior results were negative or it has been more than 10 years since last screening.

### Additional Information:
- Screening recommendations are subject to change based on individual health factors and guidelines from health authorities.
